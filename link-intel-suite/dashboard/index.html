SEO Conflict Detection
Keyword Cannibalization
Pages competing for the same terms — diluting link equity and rankings

Cannibalization Severity

Understanding Cannibalization

When two pages target the same keywords, search engines struggle to determine which to rank. This dilutes link equity and weakens both pages in SERPs.

>70% overlap — Critical: consolidate or differentiate immediately
55–70% overlap — High: review and differentiate keyword targeting
45–55% overlap — Medium: monitor and differentiate where possible
