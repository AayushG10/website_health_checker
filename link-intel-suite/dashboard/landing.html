Core Features

Everything your SEO team needs.
Nothing you don't.

Built on deterministic graph algorithms — no black boxes, no hallucinated data. Every insight is traceable back to your crawl export.

⬡

Internal Link Graph

Full graph analysis from your crawl export — broken links, orphan pages, redirect chains, nofollow attributes, crawl depth, and avg. inlinks per page.

◎

Anchor Text Intelligence

Classify every internal anchor as descriptive, generic, empty/image-only, or over-optimised. Spot exact-match stuffing before it becomes a penalty signal.

🗂️

Topical Cluster Mapping

TF-IDF + Jaccard similarity groups pages into semantic clusters with hub/scattered authority ratings. Auto-named from top keywords — no model required.

⚡

PageRank Simulation

Damping factor 0.85, 30 iterations. Shows which pages accumulate link equity and which are starved — so you know exactly where to concentrate internal links.

◆

AI Link Recommendations

Ollama-powered contextual suggestions with AI-written anchor text. Falls back to TF-IDF candidates when no model is available — the pipeline never breaks.

⬟

Health Score + Grade

A weighted formula across 7 signals produces a 0–100 score and A–F grade. Priority fix queue ranks every issue by impact so you know exactly what to fix first.
